Welcome to SinhaToons!

SinhaToons is Sri Lanka's premier destination for Sinhala dubbed cartoons. We grew up watching legendary cartoons like Soora Pappa, Koombichchi, and Pissu Poosa. Our mission is to bring that nostalgia back and provide a high-quality platform for the new generation to enjoy these classics.

Our Passion

We love cartoons! Every show on our site is handpicked to ensure it brings joy, laughter, and good values to our audience.

Community

We are building a community of cartoon lovers. Join us, share your favorite moments, and request new shows!
